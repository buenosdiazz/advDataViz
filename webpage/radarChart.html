Comparing Award Winning Actors

This chart represents the award counts for critically acclaimed actors Meryl Streep, Robert De Niro, and Daniel Day-Lewis. This radar chart demonstrates that Meryl Streep has the most awards in almost all award categories with 3 Oscars, 9 Golden Globes, and 3 Emmys. Source
