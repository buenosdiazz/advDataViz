Annual Top 20 Movies By Rating (1985-2017)

This chart demonstrates the ratings of the top 20 movies for each year from 1985-2017. Some interesting takeaways from the chart:

  • There have been no G-rated movies in the top twenty since 2011.
  • In 1992, 14 of the top 20 movies were R-rated films
  • In 2002, there were no R-rated movies in the top 20
  • Since the late 1990s, on average the number of PG-13 movies in the top 20 has increased
