Bourne Movie Series Bubble Chart

An example goes here.

This bubble chart compares the different movies in the Bourne series by Rotten Tomato score and gross ticket sales in millions of US dollars. The size of the bubbles represents the amount of money each film made. As you can see, The Bourne Ultimatum has the largest bubble an made the most box office revenue with $227,471,070. Source

Javascript library used to build this chart: flotr2
