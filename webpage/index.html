ABOUT THIS SITE

You need a browser which supports CSS Grid! Why not download one which does?

I am a graduate student at the University of Miami pursuing my MFA in Interactive Media. This semester I am taking an Interactive Data Vizualization course - This site is a portfolio for my work in this class.

Over the course of the semester we have explored various javascript libraries related to working with data objects and creating data visualizations such as underscore.js, flot.js, flotr2.js, leaflet.js, and d3.js.
